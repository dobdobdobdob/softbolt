# ⚡ Softbolt
*Artist-owned music streaming — think Substack, but for musicians.*

## The Problem
Spotify and Apple Music commoditize artists into playlists. Artists have no direct relationship with their listeners, no control over their experience, and no ownership of their audience. Substack solved this for writers. Softbolt solves it for musicians.

## The Product
Each artist gets their own branded streaming page — their music, their design, their audience. Fans subscribe directly to artists, not to a platform.

- Artist-owned streaming pages with custom branding
- Direct artist-to-fan subscription model
- Clean, embeddable music player per artist
- Built for independent and emerging artists

## Why I Built This
I previously built and shipped Humbolt, a B2C music streaming platform that grew to 50,000 MAU in 3 months and was acquired within 3 years. Softbolt is the next evolution of that thesis — built using modern AI-native tools to move faster and validate the creator-owned model in music.
